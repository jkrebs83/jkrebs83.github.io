SERVICES

I offer a variety of fresh and healthy customized meal plans, event catering services, personalized cooking classes, and more.

In-home

I can come to your home every week, or every once in a while, to cook a satisfying multi-course plated dinner or a week's worth of meals for your family. I handle the meal planning, grocery shopping, prepping and cooking, then portion and package your meals, leaving your fridge stocked and your kitchen spotless.

Delivery

I can deliver fully-prepared meals to you, tailored to your dietary needs and preferences. I can email you a customized menu each week to choose from, and typically deliver Sunday and Monday evenings. I will label and store your food and offer reheating instructions where necessary. I offer a variety of meal plan package options to choose from. Perfect for busy families or anyone who doesn't have time to cook!

Event Catering

Whether you're hosting an intimate dinner, brunch buffet, or cocktail party at your home, or having an off-site event such as a baby shower or birthday party, I can work with you to plan a customized menu, and if need be, hire an assistant and server to execute your event flawlessly.

Cooking Classes

Want to learn basic knife skills? Impress a date? Host a cooking party? I can teach you and your family or a group of friends the basics of cooking and how to plan, prepare, cook and plate a wide variety of dishes--from appetizers to entrees to desserts (including vegetarian, dairy-free, or gluten-free options). Contact me to set up your customized cooking class.

Food Styling

I offer a full range of food photography services for advertising, product development, cookbooks, websites, and any printed media.

I welcome projects of any size and scale, and also am able to accommodate a variety of requirements: From fully setup commercial kitchen and studio with food preparation and styling to a simple location photo shoot.

A quick and efficient styling service designed to help new or existing businesses, in the food industry, get a new up-to-date look to enter this highly competitive industry with a strong visual presentation of their products and services.
