About

Hi, Welcome to my site. My name is Jillian Krebsbach and I am a chef that specializes in making health supportive, plant-based meals that taste great and help you feel your best.

After graduating from the Natural Gourmet Institute, the nation's premier health-supportive culinary, I have cooked in some of New York City's best restaurants from the large James Beard award-winning farm to table vegan-inspired ABCV by Michelin star Chef Jean-Georges to the delicious, market driven-dishes in the heart of the West Village at Chef Dan Kluger's restaurant Loring Place. I also have event catering experience, and I am trained and certified in food safety.

I will customize your meals according to your dietary needs and preferences. I can create a satisfying and balanced dairy-free, gluten-free, vegetarian or vegan menu. I am committed to using the freshest ingreients, including organic produce, whole grains, grass-fed meats, and wild, sustainable seafood. I love combining seasonal food with international flavors and spices, from Southeast Asian to Mediterranean.

I will save you valuable time--menu planning, grocery shopping, cooking, portioning and packaging meals, and cleaning up afterwards. I can create a week's worth of all-natural meals and snacks for your busy family, or a plated four-course dinner for a special occasion. It's up to you!

In addition to personal chef services, I am also available for private cooking classes and events, from casual brunches to elegant cocktail parties. Thanks for visiting. Sign up for my newsletter for events, periodic kitchen tips, recipes and to stay connected!

Peaches and Cream Ice Cream

Here is a delicious and simple ice cream you can make this summer that is vegan and tastes delicious. It uses fresh fruit for flavor instead of artificial flavorings.

Prep time: 15 mins

Total time: 3 hours

Servings: 6 scoops

My Vegan Ice cream base
  • 1 14 oz can of full fat coconut milk

  • 1 cup of almond millk
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 3/4 tbsp. guar gum
  • 1/4 tsp teasspoon
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest+ 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract paste
  • 1/2 cup coconut nectar
  • 2 peaches, peeled, chopped, pits removed, keep 1/2 a cup chopped pieces for topping

    Process

  1. Mix all ice cream base ingredients together in a blender until smooth. Chill base in a refrigerator covered for 2-3 hours.

  2. Pour ice cream base into an ice cream maker and follow manufacturer's instructions. Once ice cream has thickened up, remove and scoop out into a container.

  3. Top ice cream with crushed hazelnuts and fresh peach slices and enjoy!
